About

Ever since I was a little kid, I've been taking things apart to see what's inside. Whether it was "carefully" opening my parents' old VCR with a hammer, or digging into the database underneath an electronic medical records system, I’ve always been fascinated with seeing how things worked at their lowest level. Over time, this simple curiosity has developed into a real desire to understand and improve systems, as well as a great appreciation for what data can do.

Since we now live in a world filled with systems that can easily produce massive amounts of data, I want to help organizations harness these systems in order to effectively collect, visualize, analyze, and use this data to make informed decisions about how best to improve their business, and positively impact the world.

If my interests are aligned with your needs, feel free to take a look at my resume! Thanks, Chris
